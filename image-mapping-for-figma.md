## Visual Treatment Notes

**Image characteristics:**
- Authentic DIY aesthetic from 2012-2015 (pre-smartphone photography era)
- Some images have warm/yellow color cast
- Variable lighting and backgrounds
- Circuit boards, soldering, repurposed parts visible
- This is intentional — shows maker journey

**Design treatment:**
- Border radius: 8-12px
- Subtle shadow or border
- Maintain aspect ratios
- Don't over-process — keep authentic look
- Use in card grid layout: image left/top, text right/bottom

**Layout suggestion:**
```
[Image - 300-400px]  |  Project Title
                     |  Age X • Year
                     |  Short description...
```
