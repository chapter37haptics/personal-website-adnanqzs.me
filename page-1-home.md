## Design Notes

### Layout
- Full page split into 4 equal quadrants (2x2 grid)
- Each quadrant: 50% width, 50% height on desktop
- Stack vertically on mobile: Q1 → Q2 → Q3 → Q4

### Quadrant Backgrounds
- Vary slightly to create visual interest
- Suggestions:
  - Q1 (Quote): Light gray or subtle texture
  - Q2 (Photo/Bio): White
  - Q3 (Ambition): Subtle accent color tint
  - Q4 (Visual): White or image full-bleed

### Typography
- Quote: 24-32px (medium size, NOT oversized)
- Name: 28-32px bold
- Bio: 16-18px
- Ambition heading: 24-28px bold
- Ambition body: 16-18px, comfortable line-height

### Spacing
- Generous padding within each quadrant: 40-60px
- Content should breathe — avoid cramping
- Maintain balance across all 4 sections
