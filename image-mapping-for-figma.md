## Design Recommendations

**For projects with multiple images:**

**Option A: Image Carousel/Slider**
- Best for: Projects with 3+ images (Photo Frame, LED Beats Woofer, Infrared Camera, Solar Charger)
- User can swipe/click through images
- Shows image count indicator (e.g., "3/7")

**Option B: Grid Gallery**
- Show all images in small thumbnails
- Click to expand in lightbox
- Good for showing progression/multiple angles

**Option C: Featured + Thumbnails**
- First image large, others as small thumbnails below
- Clicking thumbnail swaps to large view

**Recommended approach:**
- Projects with 1-2 images: Show both side-by-side or stacked
- Projects with 3-5 images: Carousel or featured + thumbnails
- Projects with 6-7 images (Photo Frame): Grid gallery or carousel

## Visual Treatment Notes

**Image characteristics:**
- Authentic DIY aesthetic from 2012-2015
- Variable lighting, color cast, backgrounds
- Circuit boards, soldering, repurposed parts visible
- This is intentional — shows maker journey

**Design treatment:**
- Border radius: 8-12px
- Subtle shadow or border
- Maintain aspect ratios
- Don't over-process — keep authentic look
- Consider lightbox for full-size viewing
